Privacy Policy for Rudra Home Tuitions

Last Updated: 01/01/2024

Welcome to Rudra Home Tuitions! This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect,
use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website and
services, both online and offline.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information:
Name: We collect your name to personalize our communication with you.
Email Address: We use your email address for communication purposes, including sending important updates and information.
ID Proof: For offline services, we may request a valid ID proof to verify the identity of our tutors and students.

2. Usage Information:
Website Usage: We may collect information about how you interact with our website to improve user experience and optimize our services.

How We Collect Information
We collect information through:
User Input: Information provided by you through forms on our website.
Cookies: We may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
Use of Information

We use the collected information for:
Service Delivery: To provide you with personalized and effective home tuition services, both online and offline.
Communication: Sending important updates, newsletters, and responding to your inquiries.
Verification: Verifying the identity of tutors and students through provided ID proofs for offline services.

Third-Party Access
We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or for service delivery purposes. For online services, we may use secure third-party platforms to facilitate virtual tutoring sessions.
Security Measures
We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. This includes encryption, secure connections, and access controls.

Your Rights
You have the right to:
Access: Request access to your personal information.
Correction: Request correction of inaccuracies in your information.
Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information. Updates to the Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. Check the "Last Updated" date for the latest version.

Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at rudrahometuitions@gmail.com
